Regios Team Kick Boxing

Publicado el 14-01-2022

This section will show one of my biggest personal achievments. Regios Team Kick Boxing was born in 2021 January 21, because of the purpose of promote the kick boxing in Nuevo Leon. We have been part of the history of kick boxing in Nuevo León by being participants of some important events like the "Macro Regional del Norte WAKO" tournament and the "Torneo Nacional WAKO 2021" tournament and got the 60th place of 100th in nacional level and 15th place in regional level.
